## The intent machine

Meta finds people who *might* want your app. Google finds people who are **actively looking for it.** That's the fundamental difference, and it's a powerful one.

When someone searches "best budget app" or "habit tracker for beginners," they're telling Google exactly what they need. Google Ads lets you show up at that exact moment.

## Google Ads campaign types

Google offers several campaign types, each with different strengths:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Search Ads" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Text ads that appear in Google Search results. Best for **high-intent users** actively searching for a solution your app provides.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Display Ads" icon="image">
    Visual ads across 35M+ websites and apps in Google's Display Network. Best for **awareness and retargeting** at scale.
  </Card>
  <Card title="App Campaigns (UAC)" icon="mobile">
    Automated campaigns that promote your app across Search, Play Store, YouTube, and Display â€” all at once. **Best for app installs.**
  </Card>
  <Card title="YouTube Ads" icon="play">
    Video ads on YouTube. Best for **storytelling and brand building** with a massive video audience.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  **For app developers, start with App Campaigns (Universal App Campaigns / UAC).** They're specifically designed for app installs, and Google automates most of the targeting and bidding. You just provide creative assets and a budget.
</Tip>

## How Google Ads differ from Meta

| | Google Ads | Meta Ads |
|--|-----------|---------|
| **Targeting model** | Keyword/intent-based | Interest/behavior-based |
| **User mindset** | "I need something" | "I'm browsing" |
| **Best for** | High-intent discovery | Broad awareness + retargeting |
| **Creative focus** | Ad copy and keywords | Visual creative and video |
| **Learning curve** | Medium-high | Medium |
| **Minimum budget** | $10/day recommended | $5/day minimum |

## Typical costs

| Campaign Type | Cost Range | Metric |
|--------------|-----------|--------|
| App Campaigns | $0.50 - $4.00 | Per install |
| Search Ads | $0.50 - $3.00 | Per click |
| Display Ads | $0.50 - $2.00 | Per click |
| YouTube Ads | $0.02 - $0.10 | Per view |

<Note>
  Google Ads costs vary widely by industry and competition. Finance and insurance keywords can cost $10+ per click, while niche app categories might be under $1. These ranges are general guidelines for app advertising.
</Note>
